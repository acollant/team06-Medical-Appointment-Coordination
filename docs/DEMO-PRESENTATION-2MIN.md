# Our Clinic — 2-Minute Demo Presentation

**Audience:** Client / class / stakeholders  
**Duration:** ~2 minutes (~280 words)  
**Run demo:** `./run-demo.sh` → http://localhost:8080

---

## Slide 1 — The problem (0:00–0:25)

**On screen:** Phone icon + three clinic locations (Brooklyn, Midtown, Queens)

**Say:**

> Booking a doctor visit today is fragmented. Patients call IVR menus, wait on hold, and still don't know which location has the next open slot. Staff juggle phone calls, walk-ins, and schedules across multiple sites — and reminders are easy to miss.

---

## Slide 2 — Our idea (0:25–0:50)

**On screen:** Logo **Our Clinic** + four personas (Patient, Provider, Front desk, Admin)

**Say:**

> **Our Clinic** is one coordinated system for the whole practice. Patients book online or on mobile. Staff see one live schedule. Providers manage their calendar. Admins track utilization — all tied to real appointment-call patterns from healthcare booking data.

---

## Slide 3 — Live demo highlights (0:50–1:40)

**On screen:** Split view — mobile chat on left, desk schedule on right

**Say:**

> Let me show you three moments.
>
> **First — Maria on mobile.** She opens the chatbot, books a general doctor visit in seconds, or asks for the nearest cardiologist with an open slot.
>
> **Second — when there's no in-person availability.** She tries Skin Care. No slots? The app doesn't dead-end — it lets her **call the doctor** or **book an online video visit** instead.
>
> **Third — the clinic side.** Elena at the front desk checks patients in and sends reminders. Maria gets a confirmation and a 24-hour reminder automatically.

---

## Slide 4 — Why it matters (1:40–2:00)

**On screen:** One line — *Fewer calls. Fuller schedules. Happier patients.*

**Say:**

> One platform replaces phone tag with self-service booking, smart fallbacks, and automated reminders — so the clinic runs smoother and patients get care faster. Questions?

---

## One-liner (elevator pitch)

**Our Clinic** connects patients, providers, and front desk in one appointment system — book by chat, find the nearest doctor, fall back to telehealth when slots are full, and never miss a reminder.
