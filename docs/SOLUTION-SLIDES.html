Our Clinic — Medical Appointment Coordination
Team 06 · Problem & Vision
Slide 1 / 5

The problem

  • Patients rely on phone/IVR — long holds, unclear availability, dead ends when a specialty is full
  • Multi-site clinics (Brooklyn, Midtown, Queens) — hard to find the nearest open slot
  • Front desk juggles walk-ins, phone bookings, and reminders without one live view
  • Missed appointments when confirmations and reminders are manual

Our vision

One coordinated platform for patients, providers, front desk, and admin — aligned with real healthcare appointment-call data (Kaggle 2024).

Key design decisions

  • Empty in-person search → telehealth (not a dead end)
  • Same booking pipeline for in-person and video visits
  • Async notifications — booking succeeds if email queue is down

Core domain model

EntityResponsibility
ServiceCardio, General Doctor, Skin Care (patient-friendly labels)
SlotBookable unit — modality: in-person or telehealth
AppointmentStatus + booking_channel (web, phone, mobile_chat, telehealth)
ProviderSpecialty, location, phone, telehealth enabled
NotificationLogConfirmation & reminder audit trail
CallRecordPhone/IVR transcripts (Kaggle alignment)
