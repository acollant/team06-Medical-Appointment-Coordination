Client demo guide

Medical Appointment Coordination · Our Clinic · Phase 0 prototype

Run locally: ./run-demo.sh from the repo root
Opens http://localhost:8080 automatically

Recommended demo flow (~10 min)

1Patient books by service — click Maria · Patient

  • Dashboard shows upcoming cardio visit + clinic stats
  • + Book new appointment → By service tab
  • Select General Doctor → toggle Nearest first → 5 open slots across Brooklyn & Queens
  • Click Book on any slot → confirm booking

2Closest practitioner — still as Maria

  • Open Closest to me tab → select Heart / Cardio
  • ZIP 11201 (Brooklyn) → Find closest practitioner
  • Highlights Dr. James Chen (~0.3 km) with next open slot + alternatives

3Front desk operations — sign out → click Elena · Front desk

  • Today's schedule — 11 appointments across providers (check-in, walk-ins, open slots)
  • Call log — 5 phone/IVR transcripts from Kaggle dataset; filter by book/cancel/reschedule
  • Click Match availability on a booking call to jump to patient search

4Provider view — sign out → Dr. Chen · Provider

  • Calendar shows Maria (scheduled) and John (checked in) — click to mark complete / no-show

5Admin overview — sign out → David · Admin

  • Clinic — 3 locations (Brooklyn, Midtown, Queens), appointment types, holidays
  • Reports — utilization 85%, no-show 12%, channel breakdown
